Newcomer Candidates: Characterizing Contributions of a Novice Developer to GitHub

What is this?

Hello, I'm IFraz Rehman, Doctoral course 1st year student of Software Engineering Laboratory, Nara Institute of Science & Technology (NAIST), Nara, Japan. Together with my research team, our goal is to study the on-boarding and sustainability of newcomers to Open Source projects on GitHub.

This project has 2-phases. In the phase-1 as a part of the "Registered Report track at ICSME 2020, we present our proposal with four research question to track and characterize newcomer candidate contributions on GitHub. Details about the report is available at this link. In the second phase which is complete version of our study includes the results achieved for our research questions in the respect of tracking and characterizing newcomer candidates contributions on GitHub.
